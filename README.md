# LegalActivities

LegalActivities is an iOS app that lets you create custom driving routes, set checkpoints, and time your runs. Challenge your friends, compare your best times, and become the king of your local roads!

---

## About The Project

Ever wanted to turn your daily commute or a scenic drive into a personal time trial? LegalActivities gives you the tools to do just that. Define your own "tracks" on real roads, mark your start, finish, and checkpoints, and race against the clock.

This app is perfect for:

- Friendly competition among car clubs or groups of friends  
- Practicing and improving your driving consistency on a specific route  
- Discovering and sharing the best driving roads in your area  

---

## Key Features

- **Route Creator:** Use an interactive map to drop pins, creating a custom route with a start, multiple checkpoints, and a finish line.  
- **Race Mode:** Time your drive through your saved routes, with the app automatically detecting when you pass through each checkpoint.  
- **Leaderboards(WIP):** See how your times stack up against your friends for each route.  
- **Profile & History(WIP):** Keep track of all your completed runs, personal bests, and route creations.  
- **Social Sharing(WIP):** Share your routes and your best times with friends directly.
